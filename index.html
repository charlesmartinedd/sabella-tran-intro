Professional Journey

From Dream School to Impact

Sabella's path through public health has always centered on community. From her undergraduate work at UC Irvine to achieving her dream of attending UCLA for her MPH, every step has been guided by a passion for underserved populations.

  • BS Public Health Sciences, UC Irvine
  • MPH Community Health Sciences, UCLA
  • Clinical experience at ENT clinic & CVS pharmacy

A Surprising Skill

A Green Thumb & Thoughtful Gifts

In Sabella's culture, gifting fruits is a meaningful tradition. Her family grows exotic fruits like dragonfruit, soursop, and water apple. While she admits she's "a terrible gift giver," she's found her own way to share love—handmade flower bouquets.

  • Family tradition of growing exotic fruits
  • Creates beautiful homemade flower arrangements
  • Gift-giving rooted in cultural connection

What Energizes Her

A Cat-Obsessed Heart

In 2020, Sabella started fostering cats to fill her time at home. Then in 2022, fate intervened—two tiny kittens in a cardboard box at Home Depot. Three years later, they're still her constant companions.

  • Started fostering cats in 2020
  • Adopted two Home Depot rescue kittens in 2022
  • Volunteered at LA cat shelter to stay connected
  • "Cats over dogs, always"
